Welcome Shaheed-e-Azam Sardar Bhagat Singh Inter College

Shaheed-e-Azam Sardar Bhagat Singh Inter College is a oldest school with a campus in Gonda. Our program covers Mathematics & Biology. The school was established in 1926.
We provide quality education, from an early age, to elementary school to middle school, designed to be empathetic, capable, confident, and ready for life. They graduated from high school, college and beyond.
